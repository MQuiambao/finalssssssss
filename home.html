Welcome to Augmented Reality!


A technology known as augmented reality (AR) projects digital content, such pictures, movies, or three-dimensional (3D) models, This technology uses digital content—which is usually viewed through a smartphone or specialized AR headset—overlay the real world to improve the user's impression of it. Augmented Reality (AR) technology offers immersive and interactive experiences across a range of industries, including gaming, education, healthcare, retail, and marketing.

LOCATION-BASED AR


Also known as markerless, position-based, or geo-based AR, is a type of AR that activates based on your physical location. Unlike marker-based AR which relies on scanning QR codes or specific images, location-based AR uses your device's GPS, compass, and other sensors to pinpoint your whereabouts and trigger AR experiences.

IKEA PLACE AR(Android, iOS)


Free augmented reality (AR) app that allows you to virtually place IKEA furniture in your home before you buy it. This can be a great way to see how furniture will look in your space and ensure that it fits the way you want it to. The app is available for both Android and iOS devices.
