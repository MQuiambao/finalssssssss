Applications for Augmented Reality

Pokemon Go

In AR mode, Pokémon are aware of your movement, so proceed with caution. Move toward a Pokémon too fast and you’ll scare it away...but approach slowly, and you’ll have a better chance at catching it.AR mode uses Pokémon GO’s augmented reality features to allow Pokémon to appear in and around the real-world environment right in front of you

Quiver:3D coloring

Quiver is the app you're looking for when it comes to 3D coloring with Augmented Reality (AR). It lets you transform physical coloring pages into interactive 3D experiences!Quiver is a fantastic way to add a whole new dimension to coloring, making it engaging and exciting for everyone. It's also a great introduction to the world of AR.

Trying on clothes and shoes

Augmented reality (AR) is making waves in the fashion world, and trying on clothes and shoes virtually is becoming increasingly popular!Several apps use AR technology to give you a virtual try-on experience from the comfort of your home. These apps use your phone's camera to superimpose realistic 3D models of clothing and shoes onto your body.
